The Best Movies According to Lawrence

My top 3 movies (today)

Pulp Fiction

The first Tarantino movie I ever watched. This movie pushed the boundaries of on-screen storytellingand did it while being unapologetically cool

Forest Gump

In my opinion, this is a perfect movie. It has everything in it; love, humor, history and war

Boyz n the Hood

A realistic snapshot of 90's gang culture in Los Angeles and how it affects the community as a whole. Also my favorite Hohn Singleton film.
